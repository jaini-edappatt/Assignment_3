Notice

An "advertisement notice" or "advertisement" is a public announcement, often paid, designed to promote a product, service, event, or job, aiming to inform or persuade the audience. Public notice, Implied notice, Actual notice, and Constructive notice are 4 different types of true notice drafts in India. Notice writing represents a formal written message. Web Page

A website is a collection of interconnected web pages, identified by a common domain name, that are published on one or more web servers and accessible to users via the internet. Websites come in various forms, including e-commerce, portfolio, blog, business, educational, nonprofit, forum, social media, entertainment, event, landing page, news, and membership websites, each serving a specific purpose.
